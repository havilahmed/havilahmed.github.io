Board/Directors

Dr. Abraham Yarney, PhD

Dr Abe Yarney is a licensed Professional Engineer with over ten years of experience in Traffic Engineering and Transportation Planning. He holds a Ph.D. in Transportation Engineering from the University of Florida, an MSc from the University of Leeds, UK, and a BSc in Civil Engineering from KNUST, Ghana.

Dr Yarney has a strong background in traffic operations analysis, travel demand modeling, and traffic safety analysis. He is certified in Traffic Safety Analysis and Traffic Engineering Studies in Texas. His technical expertise spans predictive safety modeling using IHSDM, ISATe, SPICE, and other HSM tools; crash data analysis and dashboarding with Python and PowerBI; and travel demand modeling using TransCAD. He is also proficient in traffic operations tools such as Synchro, VISSIM, VISUM, HCS, and CAP-X. Currently serving as a Traffic Engineer at STV in Austin, TX, Dr. Yarney is involved in high-impact projects such as the SH-45 Tolled Road Extension, Frankford Road Widening, and the California High Speed Rail Project. His role includes safety analysis, demand modeling, intersection performance studies, and preparation of design memos and reports.

Prior to STV, he served as Senior Transportation Engineer at Atkins, leading traffic forecasting and safety studies across multiple states. His earlier roles at CHW Professional Consultants and DRMP in Florida included traffic impact assessments and roadway design.

He brings a data-driven, results-oriented approach to transportation challenges, delivering practical solutions to improve roadway safety and efficiency.

In addition to his extensive expertise in traffic analysis, travel demand modeling, and traffic safety, Mr Yarney is deeply passionate about cocoa farming.

He is committed to supporting cocoa farmer education, particularly in strategies to increase yield and diversify income sources.

He also takes keen interest in cocoa marketing, with a focus on building and improving relationships with stakeholders in the cocoa industry.

Also, his expertise in traffic operations and analysis, predictive safety modeling, and travel demand forecasting can support the efficient planning and management oil and gas trading.

Prof. Komla Dzigbede

Komla D. Dzigbede is an associate professor and Chair of the Department of Public Administration at the State University of New York at Binghamton. Komla is an expert in state and local public finance and international economic policy. His research is of high value to local, state, and national governments and spans a variety of topics, including debt issuance, debt refinancing, budget system effectiveness, tax reform, financial market regulation, and sustainable economic development. Komla has extensive experience in the international public sector from his prior work as an economist at the Central Bank of Ghana and as a consultant for an international development agency. He has bachelor's and master's degrees in economics from the University of Ghana and a doctorate in public policy from the Georgia State University, United States. Komla is the editor-in-chief of the Public Finance & Management journal.

Given his background, Prof. Dzigbede's experise is highly relevant to the company's mandate and Ghana's cocoa sector at large, particularly in the areas of financial planning, sustainable development, and economic policy design for cocoa farming communities.

His understanding of tax systems, budget efficiency, and debt strategies can support the company's financial health as well the efforts to improve financial inclusion and long-term planning for cocoa farmers.

Additionally, his insight into public finance and international economic policy can inform strategies that enhance the company's activities in the cocoa sector and ensure more stable, resilient income sources for farmers.

Dr. Albert Sey (MD)

Dr. Albert Sey is a board-certified cardiologist with Southern Indiana Heart and Vascular. Albert Sey has been practicing medicine since 2005 after completing medical school in Ghana. He's had diverse experience in various specialties during his practice as a family/general physician. Between 2010 and 2020, his interest has been focused on evidence-based medicine and has since obtained board certification in internal medicine, cardiovascular medicine, and electrophysiology. Dr. Sey is currently focused on delivering quality care to patients with heart rhythm abnormalities by treatment with medications, cardiac devices, and performing invasive surgeries for arrhythmias. He had his fellowship in cardiac electrophysiology from Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center, Dartmouth University, and did his residency in internal medicine at Cleveland Clinic.

Dr Sey brings not only his professional expertise but also a personal passion for cocoa farming and the impact of cocoa on health.

He is very committed to ensuring that cocoa beans are of highest quality to protect the well-being of consumers.

With his medical background, he advocates for safe and hygienic handling practices and supports efforts that link cocoa quality to long-term public health benefits.

His unique perspective helps bridge the gap between agriculture and health in the cocoa value chain.

When it comes to oil and gas, Dr Sey is particularly passionate about sourcing clean and high- quality refined petroleum products. He is passionate about promoting a culture of health- conscious operations across the industry.

Obed Effah

Legal Counsel - Lexnak Corporation

Obed Effah is a seasoned attorney licensed in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, bringing extensive legal expertise to his role as Legal Counsel at Lexnak Corporation. He is also the founder and managing partner of the Law Office of Obed Effah, a respected law firm based in Lowell, Massachusetts, established in 2017.

Attorney Effah began his academic journey in Ghana, where he earned a bachelor's degree in Political Science and Economics. He later pursued legal studies in the United States, obtaining his Juris Doctor and gaining admission to the Massachusetts Bar in 2017. Since then, he has been dedicated to delivering exceptional legal services.

At his firm, Attorney Effah handles a wide range of legal matters, with a focus on transactional advisory and civil litigation. Known for his professionalism, empathy, and clear communication, he has built a strong reputation among clients and peers alike.

In his role at Lexnak Corporation—a company operating in the commodities sector with a focus on cocoa, oil, and gas—Attorney Effah provides strategic legal guidance on transactional and compliance matters. His international background and deep legal insight are key to navigating the complex regulatory frameworks of global commodity markets. His comprehensive understanding of the legal landscape in sectors such as cocoa and energy further strengthens his role as a trusted legal advisor.
